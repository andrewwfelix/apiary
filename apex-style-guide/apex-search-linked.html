The process

Four steps. Zero risk.

A straightforward model built on transparency — you see exactly what we measured, and exactly what changed.

01 — Baseline
We snapshot your site
We pull your current traffic, keyword rankings, and conversion data from Google Analytics and Search Console to establish a clear starting point.
02 — Audit & Strategy
We find the gaps
A full technical crawl with Screaming Frog, competitive research via Semrush, and a prioritised action plan targeting your highest-impact opportunities.
03 — Execution
We do the work
On-page optimisation, technical fixes, backlink strategy, and content improvements — deployed directly via GitHub and Netlify where applicable.
04 — Proof & Invoice
You see results first
At 90 days, we produce a before/after report from your live analytics data. When agreed thresholds are met, you receive the invoice. No results, no charge.
